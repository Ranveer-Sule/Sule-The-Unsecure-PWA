Privacy Policy

What information we collect: your username, your password (stored hashed with bcrypt, never in plain text), your date of birth (used only to confirm you are 13 or older), a two-factor authentication secret (stored encrypted), and backup recovery codes (stored hashed).

Feedback: any feedback you submit is stored anonymously. It is not linked to your account or any other personal information, so we cannot identify who left a particular piece of feedback.

Why we collect it: to create and secure your account. We do not share or sell your data.

How long we keep it: we retain your account information for as long as your account is active, and delete it when you delete your account. Private error logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days and then cleared. Anonymous feedback may be retained to help improve the service.

How we protect it: your data is transmitted over HTTPS, passwords and recovery codes are hashed, and two-factor secrets are encrypted at rest.

Your rights: you can view the data we hold about you and permanently delete your account at any time from your My Data page. Because feedback is stored anonymously, it cannot be individually identified or removed once submitted.
